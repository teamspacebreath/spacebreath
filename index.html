About us

We’re Harry and Jonas - two friends who met years ago while traveling. A few lucky coincidences led us to make music together. Harry brings deep experience and a lifelong passion for sound.

In recent years, our focus has shifted inward. We’ve explored practices that bring us a little closer to our true selves - an ongoing journey of self-discovery. Breathwork became essential, and together with our love for music, we created SpaceBreath.

Now we’re sharing this with you. We create content to support your inner journey - and, hopefully, leave the world a little better than we found it.
