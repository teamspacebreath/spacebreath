Intro

We’re Harry and Jonas - two friends who met years ago while traveling. A few lucky coincidences led us to make music together. Harry brings deep experience and a lifelong passion for sound.

In recent years, our focus has shifted inward. We’ve explored practices that bring us a little closer to our true selves - an ongoing journey of self-discovery. Breathwork became essential, and together with our love for music, we created SpaceBreath. Now we’re sharing this with you. We create content to support your inner journey - and, hopefully, leave the world a little better than we found it.

Work

Breathwork

Experience the power of conscious breathing with our guided breathwork sessions on YouTube. Led by Jonas and thoughtfully produced by our team, each session is designed to help you reduce stress, sharpen focus, and reconnect with the present moment: wherever you are.

Music

The heartbeat of SpaceBreath. Every track is composed by us to support your inner journey and deepen your breathwork experience.

About

We’re Harry and Jonas, two friends brought together by a shared love for creativity. Out of our passion for music and mindfulness, we started SpaceBreath, a project dedicated to making breathwork accessible, immersive, and inspiring.

On our YouTube channel, Jonas writes the scripts, guides each session with his voice, and leads the practice as the instructor. Harry composes original music, blending ambient electronic sounds, natural environments, and unique samples to give each session its own atmosphere. Together, we reimagine traditional breathwork techniques, adding our own spin to create experiences that feel fresh, calming, and transformative.

SpaceBreath is a fully self-produced project, and we’re lucky to have our friend Erich on board to bring it all to life visually with his beautiful graphic design.

Our goal is simple: to help you create space in your mind, reduce stress, and reconnect with yourself - through breath, music, and creativity.
